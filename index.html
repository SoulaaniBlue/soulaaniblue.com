The Story

"I love you, still" is my rawest exploration of love's persistence. These tracks navigate the space between what was and what remains, honoring love that endures beyond circumstance.

Recorded, mixed, and mastered entirely by me, this EP represents complete artistic control and vision. Every sound, every vocal, every moment is intentionally crafted.
